About vaping


Tradition since 1889

The concept of vaping, or inhaling vaporized substances, has been around for centuries. However, modern vaping as we know it, particularly with electronic cigarettes (e-cigarettes), began to gain popularity in the early 2000s. The modern e-cigarette was invented by a Chinese pharmacist named Hon Lik in 2003. Lik's motivation for inventing the e-cigarette was personal; he wanted to find a less harmful alternative to smoking traditional cigarettes after his father, a heavy smoker, died of lung cancer. After the invention of the e-cigarette, vaping devices started to become more widely available in the early to mid-2000s, primarily in China.
